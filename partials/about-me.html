About Me

Derek Tang is an Investment Banking Associate at Bank of America Merrill Lynch (BofAML), focused primarily on advising large-cap corporate clients in the Engineering & Industrials sectors. He joined BofAML in 2010 as an Analyst and was promoted to associate after three years in the global banking and markets analyst program at BofAML with experiences in Sales & Trading, Credit & Market Risk and Capital Markets. Mr. Tang has 7 years of professional experience in Banking and Consulting.

Prior to joining BofAML, Mr. Tang was a consulting analyst at Accenture, covering system integration & technology clients such as Oracle and SAP. Mr. Tang graduated from Columbia University with a B.S. in Applied Mathematics and holds a M.S. in Engineering from University of Pennsylvania. Mr. Tang also has native proficiency in Chinese Mandarin.
