About Me Learning and Growing

Thanks for reading this. After graduating from UPenn with a degree in engineering, I have worked in Bank of America Merrill Lynch and Accenture.

Being an investment banker at Bank of America Merrill Lynch was both challenging and rewarding, however, my passion for creativity and innovation wasn't satisfied. Since I have always loved the idea of building web applications, I started exploring a career in web development and taught myself basic Ruby and Javascript, with a bit of HTML5 and CSS.

The more I played around and learned, the more I became passionate about creating beautiful web applications that strive to enlighten, solve complex problems, and push innovation. So I'd like to be at that point where creativity meets functionality. I'd like to be creative, solve problems and bring those solutions to others.
